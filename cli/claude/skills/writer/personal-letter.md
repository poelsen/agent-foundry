# Personal / informal letter

Register: warm, informal, first name, contractions welcome, loose structure. The trap here is AI over-formality and generic warmth, so guard against both.

## Structure

- Open naturally, the way you'd actually start writing to this person, not "I am writing to inform you."
- **Anchor in one or two concrete shared details**: a named event, an inside reference, something only the two of you would write. Generic warmth ("I hope you are doing wonderfully") reads as hollow and machine-made. If the user hasn't supplied a concrete detail, ask for one, a personal letter without particulars isn't personal.
- Loose structure is fine; follow the thought.
- Close warmly and plainly.

## What to avoid

- Business scaffolding (subject lines, numbered points, "per our conversation").
- Rule-of-three flourishes ("healthy, happy, and fulfilled").
- The manufactured-sentiment register. Specific beats effusive: "the way you handled the toast at Anna's wedding" beats "your wonderful, caring nature."
